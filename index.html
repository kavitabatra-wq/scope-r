SCOPE⁠-⁠R

Survey-based Collaborative Opportunities & Projects Enabling Research

A pilot program from the Office of Research providing selected trainees and faculty with institutional access to the Qualtrics Market Research panel — so you can reach the right respondents and collect rigorous survey data.

$0
Free Qualtrics panel & online-sample access under the School’s retained agreement. No cash award and no cost to you — no quote, no purchase, no budget to prepare.
Read the program guidelines (PDF) → Start your application

Before you apply — eligibility at a glance

  • Open to medical trainees (students, residents, fellows) and faculty in good standing; trainees name a faculty mentor.
  • The project must be survey-based, using Qualtrics for instrument deployment and/or respondent recruitment.
  • IRB approval (or a formal exemption determination) is required at the time of application.
  • You apply with a single PDF application packet (project narrative + IRB approval letter + PI/mentor support letter; department-chair letter for faculty-initiated projects), plus a CITI training certificate.
  • One active application per person/project. This is a pilot program with no cash award.
